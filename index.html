      A great feature in the KARIGAR application is that you can add pictures of your bedroom, lawn, etc. while placing a service request. Professionals can also post on timeline to showcase their work.

      In case the user has questions about the billing, rates, or anything, they can simply contact the customer executives via the given chat, email, and contact number. This is an excellent feature as users can get their issues resolved promptly.

      KARIGAR shows the rating of the service providers before users book their preferred services. The app also gives its the option of rating and providing feedback upon the completion of the service.
